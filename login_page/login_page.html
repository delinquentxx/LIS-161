What is UP
FLIPP?

UP FLIPP is an organization housed within the School of Library
and Information Science (SLIS) of the University of the Philippines
Diliman which aims to promote excellence in and awareness of the
role of librarianship in today's society through efforts such as annual
promotional activities.
